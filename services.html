Services

Because everyone deserves healthy eyes — affordable check-ups near you and glasses delivered to your door

Comprehensive eye checkup

We help you book full eye check-ups at trusted clinics near you in Kenya. During the check-up, your eyesight is tested, you get prescriptions for glasses or contact lenses if needed, and your eyes are checked for common problems like allergies, infections, and age-related issues. We also offer eye care services for companies and workplaces. Our services are affordable and open to everyone — even if you don’t have insurance — so you can keep your eyes healthy without worrying about the cost or compromising on quality.

Door-to-door delivery

We make eye care easy by delivering your glasses or contact lenses right to your doorstep. After your eye exam (or if you already have a prescription), we help you choose custom frames and lenses without the need for extra trips. Our door-to-door delivery saves you time and stress, no matter where you live. This service is loved by our clients and makes quality eye care more convenient than ever.
