About me

My name is Samuel Mbeche, a licensed optometrist with over 5 years of hands-on experience in Kenya’s leading optical and eyewear companies. I’m also an ardent eyecare ambassador, passionate about making quality vision care accessible to everyone.

Over the years, I’ve seen too many people walk away from much-needed eyewear simply because they couldn’t afford it or lacked insurance coverage. That’s why I founded this online mobile optical shop — to bridge the gap between affordability and quality, and bring customized, stylish, and functional eyewear directly to you.

We offer bespoke eyewear solutions, tailored to your prescription, style, and comfort needs — including blue light filters, photochromatic lenses, anti-glare coatings, and progressive lenses, all paired with durable, fashionable frames.

Whether you’re a student, a professional, or someone simply in need of visual clarity, this platform is built for you — no middlemen, no inflated costs, just accessible eyecare with uncompromised quality.

Welcome to a new way of seeing.
